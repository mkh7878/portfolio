In my masters degree I studied advanced programming and machine learning in audiovisual technologies. I learned to use ThreeJS, Unity, Blender and Unreal Engine for crafting immersive 3D environments, dynamic object manipulation and interactive experiences.
I designed inclusive interactions and explored alternative controllers and HCI methods.
My dissertation, Synesthetic Composition was a python application that “listened” to music as it was being played, analyzed the mood of the music and created corresponding visuals.
